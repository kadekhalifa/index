Welcome!

Thank you so much for visiting my website. I'll give you a brief overview of my history: I'm just your average small town guy with very big dreams.

I struggled with major anxiety (still do at times) and it hindered my progress in many aspects of my life. It didn't help not knowing what I wanted out of life either, but I knew that I wanted to help people and not be a "part of the machine."

My passions were software development and fitness. I was broke, but luckily, the internet is booming so hard and you can take idea's to the internet with very little cost. So, that's exactly what I did.

I was always into fitness so I started offering online personal training to help others reach their health and physique goals. I also, started consulting young up and coming entrepreneurs. Offering solutions to start their own online business and become independently successful.

The internet is so amazing considering you can literally open an eCommerce or service based store without having to spend a fortune on licensing, buildings, etc. Anyone can be an entrepreneur in today's day and age. It's really amazing when you think about it.
